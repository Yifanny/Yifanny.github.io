Research and Education

I am a Postdoc Research Fellow at the Institute for Transport Planning and Systems of ETH Zürich. My major research interests lie in autonomous driving, motion planning, car-following model, lane-change model, and intelligent transportation systems. I completed my doctoral degree under the supervision of Prof. Jianping Wang at City University of Hong Kong in Summer 2022. My doctoral thesis focuses on Human Driving Behavior Modeling and Applications in Autonomous Driving.

I obtained my M.Eng. degree and B.Sc. degree from ENSIIE, France (2014-2017) and Xidian University, China (2011-2015), respectively.
